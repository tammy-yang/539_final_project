About

Hi, my name is Tammy. I had my summer internship in Utah in 2023, and that was one of my most amazing summer. I visited several national parks and state parks in Utah, went hiking, camping, and paddleboarding. That summer was incredible and unforgettable, so I decided to jot them down as memory!

The beauty of Utah was beyond my imagination. Utah is a nature and outdoor wonderland with five well-known national parks. Only Alaska and California have more National parks than Utah. They are usually called Utah's mighty five: Bryce Canyon, Zion, Arches, Capitol Reef, and Canyonlands. These National parks are among the most visited and photographed in the worlds

The information is from nps.gov and the photos were taken by me.
